Strategic Initiatives & AI Projects

Practical recruiter-focused systems and workflows designed to improve clarity, speed, consistency, and hiring quality.

Center — AI Recruiting Assistant

I built a simple AI assistant configured to a recruiting context — including tone, company context, candidate bar, hiring principles, and role-specific non-negotiables. It is designed to support recruiters in doing higher-quality work with more consistency and less manual rewriting.

The assistant can run workflows across the hiring process, helping improve role clarity, outreach quality, intake structure, interview quality, and decision support.

/enhance /kickoff /outreach /interviewkit /okr /detox /rewrite /signals

Custom Intake Meetings

I created custom GPT-based recruiter support workflows for hiring manager intake meetings, using tools like Replit to shape a more structured process. The goal was to make role expectations clearer from the start, improve alignment between recruiters and hiring managers, and reduce confusion around scope, candidate bar, and must-have requirements.

This helps create better kickoff conversations, sharper role briefs, and more focused hiring plans before sourcing even begins.

Sourcing & Hiring Workflow Automation

I have been exploring AI-led automation to streamline sourcing, research, and screening workflows with a focus on recruiter productivity and scalable hiring practices.

This includes testing ways to reduce repetitive work, improve search quality, support recruiter judgment with better context, and build systems that can scale across multiple roles and stakeholder groups.
