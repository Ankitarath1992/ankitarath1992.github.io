Strategic Initiatives & AI Projects

Recruiter-focused systems and workflow ideas built to improve clarity, speed, and hiring quality.

Center — AI Recruiting Assistant

I built a simple AI recruiting assistant tailored to recruiting workflows and context. It is designed to support better job descriptions, sharper hiring manager intake, stronger candidate outreach, more structured interview design, clearer hiring success metrics, and more consistent recruiter communication. The goal is to make recruiting work more thoughtful, consistent, and effective without losing the human side of hiring.

Custom Intake Meetings

I created custom GPT-based recruiter support workflows using Replit to improve hiring manager intake meetings. The purpose was to bring more clarity at the start of a search, define the role more precisely, align on candidate expectations earlier, and reduce confusion around what “good” looks like before sourcing begins.

Sourcing & Hiring Workflow Automation

I have been exploring AI-led automation to streamline sourcing, research, and screening workflows. This work is focused on improving recruiter productivity, reducing repetitive manual work, and building more scalable hiring processes that can support multiple roles and stakeholder groups with better consistency.
